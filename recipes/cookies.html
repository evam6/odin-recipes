Chocolate Chip Cookies

About

The chocolate chip cookies are a staple. They are delicious and fast and easy to make. The cookies are better the next day, so try not to eat them all right away. They can be stored for several days in a can or a jar.

Ingredients

  • 100 g butter, melted
  • 100 g brown sugar
  • 165 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 150 g chocolate, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 ËšC.
  2. Line the baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Put sugar in a large bowl.
  4. Pour in the melted butter, add the egg and whisk for a few minutes.
  5. Sift the flour, salt and baking soda and add to the bowl.
  6. Stir to combine.
  7. Add the chopped chocolate and mix it in.
  8. Create small balls from the dough (about 2 tablespoons), flatten them a bit and place on the baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 12 minutes.
