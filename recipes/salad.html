Salad

About

This tasty and refreshing salad makes for a great supper. You can also add cucumber and feta cheese to the salad. It is best served with toasted rye bread.

Ingredients

  • handful of cherry tomatoes
  • small jar of olives
  • 1 avocado
  • mixed greens
  • olive oil
  • rye bread

Steps

  1. Chop tomatoes and avocado to small pieces.
  2. Add to a bowl with mixed greens.
  3. Add olives and olive oil.
  4. Mix the salad.
  5. Serve with a loaf of rye bread.
